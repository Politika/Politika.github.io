H.R.9001

A Universal $500 Tax Rebate

Official Name: To compell the IRS to grant all citizens of the United State of America a $500 tax rebate in the interest of promoting spending.

Summary:
This law, if enacted, would essentially grant citizens free money in order to encourage spending and promote economic growth. More summary blah blah bhah.
